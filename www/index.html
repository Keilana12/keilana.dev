Isabelle Rugg

Isabelle Rugg is a highschooler living in California's Bay Area. An athlete, traveler, student, and overall curious person, she finds inspiration for design in many different settings. She isn't afraid to reach out of her comfort zone and her adventurous attitude leads to unique designs. Isabelle tends to lean toward more simplistic designs, but she enjoys a good challenge with more complicated ideas.
